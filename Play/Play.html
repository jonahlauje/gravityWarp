gravityWarp

A multiplayer first-person shooter where you can warp gravity and walk on walls!

Leverage fast-paced movement with thrilling gun combat, and use your gravity-shifting ability to access advantageous positions and surprise the enemy!

Controls: WASD to move, left click to fire, right click to ADS. Press R to reload, number keys to change weapon. Press F when near a wall to warp gravity.

Click the button to the right to go to this project's Github page. If your game isn't loading, try this link: https://jonahlauje.github.io/gravityWarp
